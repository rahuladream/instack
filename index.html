Bill Gates writes to PM, praises India's lockdown measures & Aarogya Setu app

Microsoft Co-founder Bill Gates has written to PM Narendra Modi on India's response to coronavirus, where he said, "We commend your leadership and the proactive measures you and your government have taken to flatten the curve of the COVID-19 infection rate in India, such as adopting a national lockdown." Gates also praised the government's COVID-19 contact-tracing app 'Aarogya Setu'
